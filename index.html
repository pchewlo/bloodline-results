Part II · Then vs now
Two events, six months apart
January → July: the leap

On 31 January we took a big squad to a much larger GI London and ground out a mid-pack finish. In July a leaner team turned fewer matches into more medals — and a top-four result.

Worth being straight about it: January's field was nearly twice the size — 1,870 athletes to July's 1,036 — so 16th there was earned against a lot more clubs. The fairer read is per-head, and there the story only gets better. The July squad scored almost double the points per athlete (74.6 vs 39.1) and medalled nearly one-for-one — 0.9 medals per athlete against 0.4 in January. Fewer people, sharper day. The medals got shinier too: four silvers in July after none in January.
